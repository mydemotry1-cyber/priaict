Exam Instructions
Read all rules carefully before starting
1
Read Carefully: Read all questions carefully before answering. For Match the Following, once locked, the answer cannot be changed.
⏱️
Time Limit: The exam has a 45-minute time limit. Timer shown at the top turns red at 5 minutes — auto-submits at 0:00.
4️⃣
4 Sections: MCQ, True/False, Match the Following, and Full Form. Each section has 10 questions worth 1 mark each. Complete all sections.
🖥️
Fullscreen Mode: The exam runs in fullscreen. Switching tabs or windows will generate a warning. Three violations = auto-submit.
🚫
No Refresh/Close: Do not refresh or close the browser. Your session may be lost and the exam will be submitted automatically.
🧭
Navigation: Use the Question Navigator panel to jump to any question. Arrow keys also work. Green = answered, Red = unanswered.
💾
Auto Save: Answers are automatically saved as you go. Click Submit only when you are completely done with all sections.
📊
Instant Results: After submission, you will see your complete results with section-wise marks, grade, and a detailed answer review showing right/wrong answers.
